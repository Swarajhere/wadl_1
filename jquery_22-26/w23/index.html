Student Clubs & Activities

Coding Club

Description: A club for programming enthusiasts to participate in coding competitions, hackathons, and workshops.

Achievements: Won 1st place in National Hackathon 2024, organized 5 coding workshops in 2025.

Robotics Club

Description: Focuses on building and programming robots for national and international competitions.

Achievements: 2nd place in RoboCon 2024, developed an autonomous robot for a local expo.

Dance Club

Description: A space for students to learn and perform various dance styles, from classical to contemporary.

Achievements: Performed at PICT Annual Fest 2025, won 3rd place in State Dance Competition 2024.
